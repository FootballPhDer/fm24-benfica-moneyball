The Neves question

João Neves, Javi Guerra, and Rafael Luís all land in the same cluster, close together — independent statistical confirmation that Guerra and the club's own academy prospect share Neves's on-pitch profile, separate from what FM's role stars say.

Tiago Gouveia sits at the edge of the direct-creator cluster, apart from the pack — his output this season is a statistical outlier even among attacking players.

Method: 18 per-90 and rate stats standardized, reduced via PCA (2 components, ~58% variance explained), clustered with k-means (k=6). Players with fewer than 10 apps or incomplete stats excluded. Unscouted players (name hidden by scouting knowledge) excluded from display.
